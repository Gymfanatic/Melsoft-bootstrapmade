Frequently Asked Questions

Melsoft Academy is a coding bootcamp that provides an online and oniste software Development course to technology enthuasiasts globaly.

1. Is Melsoft Academy accredited?

This is a non-accredited program.At the course completion, participants receives a Certificate of Completion , plus a robust portfolio of projects to showcase their skills to potential employers .

2. What is a bootcamp?

A coding bootcamp is a concentrated course that teaches essential skills and provides real-world training for peope seeking jobs in computer and information technology fields.

3. How many hours a day is in a coding bootcamp?

The number of hours a day you spend daily at your coding bootcamp will depend on whether you are doing a full-time or part-time bootcamp.A full-time six months bootcamp will usually include several hours of class time daiy.On the other hand , your part-time bootcamp can run for 24-weeks but fewer hours per week.

4. Do you need to know math for coding bootcamp?

Although software engineers and mathematicians share many traits like critical thinking, advanced math skills are not required for profeessionals software engineers.bootcamps are nto built around complex math sectuons , and entry-level jobs do not require equation solving.

5. Will a coding bootcamp get me a job?

Yes. Many companies will accept candidates based on skills , background, and bootcamps or projets they've completed - a degree isnt always a requirement.
